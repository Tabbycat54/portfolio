About Me

Hello there! My name is Tabitha, but most everyone knows me as Tabbycat54. I am a game master, gamer, Twitch Streamer, writer, Jazz musician and a Jill of all trades. I have been in many different roles, and I wear a lot of hats. At the end of the day I am just a girl who loves to create and explore new ideas.

Apart from being all of these things, I'm also a pet mom with way way way too many cats and a couple of dogs. I am indeed a crazy cat lady, and I embrace it fully! When I'm not busy creating content, gaming, coding, or working, you can find me spoiling my fur babies and enjoying the simple pleasures of life.

The biggest hat that I wear is that of a mental health professional. I work with teenagers, and it is the most fulfilling role I've ever had. I have been in customer service and retail, working with electronics, TV, and internet. I have been in food service as a delivery driver, cook, and food lead. I have been a social worker, and let me tell you, that's not for me. I have found my true calling as a Case Manager, and I am in school now to get a second Master's Degree in Counseling so that I can become a Licensed Professional Counselor. Second you may ask? Yes, I have a Master's Degree in Criminal Justice already, but I found that my passion lies in helping people on a more personal level. I also have two Bachelor's Degrees, one in Psychology and another in Music. I even have two minors in Sociology and Criminal Justice. I currently have certifications in Case Management, Wellness Coaching, and Python Programming. I love to learn and grow, and I believe that education is a lifelong journey.

I created this portfolio to showcase my skills and projects in web development and coding. I am excited to share my work with you and hope that you find it interesting and engaging. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!
